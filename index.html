05Sparky Mascot

Sparky is the AI assistant / copilot mascot, appearing in wizards, onboarding and marketing. "Sparky AI advisor" is an approved marketing term.

  • The classic design is the lightning bolt — 50+ designs and 9 color variants exist in the design system.
  • The corporate design is the premium platform mascot.
  • The founder avatar variant is the default guide for B2B / B2B2C users.
  • Sparky appears as a copilot in wizards, in onboarding flows, and in marketing material.

06Naming Rules

Product and company naming rules for all user-facing and marketing copy.

  • User-facing copy must say "Home Energy Hub" or "the Hub" — never the internal abbreviation.
  • Use "P1 dongle" only for generic third-party hardware, not for the Home Energy Hub.
  • ChargeBack (capital C, capital B) is the reimbursement product — never use it to mean a payment-dispute "chargeback".
  • Company name: ChargeControl B.V.
  • Taglines: "The energy operating system for electric fleets" (email) · "The Intelligent Energy Platform" (footer).
  • Legacy pre-rebrand product and company names are banned in all copy — internal codenames never appear in anything user-facing.

08Partner / Sub-brand Co-branding

ChargeControl supports partner-branded experiences (automotive, leasing and energy partners each with their own palette and logo), with strict boundaries.

  • Partner colors apply to their scoped surfaces only — ChargeControl chrome keeps the canonical palette.
  • Text colors on partner surfaces are derived automatically for contrast safety — readable text is computed from the partner primary color.
  • White-label flows may carry a partner logo and primary-color override.
  • Never recolor the ChargeControl mark with partner colors.
