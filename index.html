Licensing & attribution

All stock library images are sourced from Wikimedia Commons under open licenses (CC0 / Public Domain: no attribution required; CC BY / CC BY-SA: credit the author and link the license when publishing). Images were resized and re-encoded for web delivery. The machine-readable per-image record — source page, author, exact license and license URL — is in assets/stock/LICENSES.json?v=ae7be66212. When republishing a CC BY / CC BY-SA image anywhere (marketing pages, PDFs, decks), copy the credit line shown under its card — format: Photo: <author>, Wikimedia Commons, <license> with the author linked to the source page and the license linked to its deed.

CC BY-SA share-alike rule for modified images: if you modify a CC BY-SA image (crop, recolor, overlay text or logos, blend it into a composite), the modified image must itself be published under the same CC BY-SA license and marked as modified (e.g. “cropped from the original”). Plain resizing / re-encoding for delivery does not count as a modification. Do not fold CC BY-SA imagery into proprietary composites you cannot share-alike — pick a CC0 / Public Domain image instead. In the ChargeControl app, credits render automatically via shared/brand-stock-credits.ts (enforced by CI).

Every future icon is added through generate-icons.mjs — never drawn ad hoc. The script encodes the full recipe: 24×24 viewBox, stroke 2, round caps & joins, navy #0B2347 strokes, max one cyan #0EA5E9 accent element, white variant swaps navy→white and keeps cyan, charge motif via the shared bolt() helper (never the logo arc + bolt), naming cc-icon-<slug>.svg + -white twin. Brand Usage — Do’s & Don’ts

Do

  • Use the exact logo files from this guide — the two arcs + bolt are the mark.
  • Keep stroke width 5 and round linecaps in every reproduction.
  • Use the white monochrome variant on dark backgrounds.
  • Use Brand Cyan #0EA5E9 for CTAs, links and accents; Navy #0B2347 for headings.
  • Set headings in Outfit, body copy in Inter, numerics/code in JetBrains Mono.
  • Give the mark clear space of at least the bolt’s width on all sides.

Don’t

  • Never redraw, re-trace or approximate the mark.
  • Never omit the bolt or use a plain-bolt logo — the ring + bolt combination is the mark.
  • Never change stroke width or caps (no width 6, no butt caps).
  • Never recolor the mark outside the approved variants (primary, white, navy, cyan).
  • Never recolor the ChargeControl mark with partner colors.
  • Never use legacy blue #2563eb on branded surfaces.
  • Never stretch, rotate, outline, or add effects (shadows, gradients) to the mark.
